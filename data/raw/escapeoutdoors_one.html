Description

An everyday essential, this effortless jacket will transition seamlessly from city sidewalks to mountain trails. A piece as sophisticated as it is functional--and is designed to do double-time as a warmth-boosting layer under a shell in cooler temperatures--the Cypress Jacket will be that go-to piece you can’t believe you lived without.
An everyday essential, this effortless jacket will transition seamlessly from city sidewalks to mountain trails. A piece as sophisticated as it is functional--and is designed to do double-time as a warmth-boosting layer under a shell in cooler temperatures--the Cypress Jacket will be that go-to piece you can’t believe you lived without.

Features

  • Packable into interior left pocket. When packed, exterior loops provide backpack strap capability for effortless travel.
  • This style features ultra lightweight channel fill construction paired with lightweight fabrics and trims for weightless warmth and effortless movement.
  • Down-filled collar adds warmth and element protection. Signature double-stripe reflective details at the back add visibility in low light.
  • Removable elasticized interior backpack straps allow jacket to be carried hands-free over the shoulders
  • Articulated sleeves and underarm gussets enhance fit and add range of motion
  • Recessed Power Stretch cuffs lock in heat and keep sleeves in place during active use
